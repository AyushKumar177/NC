Multimodal LLMs
Gemini, developed by Google, is a multimodal LLM that can process and generate responses based on inputs that are not just text, but also other types of inputs such as images, code, and potentially, audio and video [5]. GPT-2
GPT-2, developed by OpenAI, is a multimodal LLM model that is said to include as many as 1.7 trillion parameters, demonstrating its extensive and profound learning capabilities [5]. It is expected to have better reasoning, problem-solving, and multi-modal capabilities.

Llama
Llama, developed by Meta, is another powerful LLM that has been making waves in the AI community. It is known for its ability to generate human-like text and has been used in various applications such as chatbots and content generation.
